about

Bloom Up is a website where people who have been victims of sexual assault, domestic violence, etc. can anonymously post their their stories. After providing specific details and a location of the assault the information can be connected to local programs that help stopping these situatuions in those areas specified.

We are all about breaking the silence!

There is no space for fear in here. We exists to fully support you and bring the help that you desperately need. We know what you're going through and we have the resources to give your life a 360 spin to bring your smile, your confidence and your life back
